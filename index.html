Building with passion. Consistently tinkering.

Constantly trying to improve myself as a developer and as a human being.

My life revolves around creating, improving, and producing everything put in front of me with purpose, quality, and pride.

There is always work on the docket, and I truly believe that it’s up to us as individuals to do all that needs to be done. Because of this, I like to make myself available to do any and all work that needs doing. This isn’t simply because it has to be done in order for professional progress or for a project to succeed, but it also allows me to learn a wide variety of skills and become something of a jack of all trades. It’s great to be able to apply yourself to any situation a team needs, and to be positively useful and beneficial to the project at hand.
